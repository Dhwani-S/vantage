Experimental Validation — Does It Actually Work?
4 experiments, 14 unit tests, real API data. Here's what we found.
Experiment 1
Information Gain Drops Only When Content Is Truly Redundant
FINDING Paraphrased text (~62% gain) stays above threshold because different words = new n-grams. Only verbatim repetition triggers convergence. Zero false positives on diverse research.
Experiment 2
Convergence Fires at the Right Time — Never Too Early
FINDING Identical content converges at step 3. Novel injection delays convergence. All-unique content never converges. The 2-consecutive-low rule prevents premature stopping.

Experiment 4
Before vs After — Wasted Iterations in Stuck Loops
FINDING In a pathological loop (agent repeating the same query), the tracker saves 2-3 iterations — a 40-60% reduction in wasted LLM calls. Legitimate research is unaffected.
